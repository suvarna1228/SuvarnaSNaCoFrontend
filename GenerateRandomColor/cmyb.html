Cyan Magenta Yellow (C M Y)

C M Y K is a subtractive reflected light color model, where each color is stored in one byte.

All possible combinations of C M Y K give 2³² different colors.

Starting with white color, to which different color inks are added to absorb (subtract) light that is reflected.
