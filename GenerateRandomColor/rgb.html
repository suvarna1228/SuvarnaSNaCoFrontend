Red Green Blue ( R G B)

R G B is an additive color projected light model where each color is stored in one byte.

All possible combinations of red, green, and blue give 16,777,216 different colors.

Starting with ( Red, Green, and Blue) = (0,0,0) (black), different color "lights" are added to produce visible colors.
